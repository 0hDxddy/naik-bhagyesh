Continental GT 650

All About the Continental GT 650

Specifications

The Continental GT 650 is a café racer with a vintage look and modern performance. It features a 648cc parallel-twin engine that produces 47 horsepower, offering a thrilling ride for those who love both speed and style.

  • Engine: 648cc, parallel-twin
  • Power: 47 bhp
  • Torque: 52 Nm
  • Price: ₹3,19,000 (Ex-showroom)
  • Fuel Tank Capacity: 12.5 liters
  • Top Speed: 160 km/h
  • Colors: Ice Queen, Dr. Mayhem, Rocker Red
